About Us


Honorato Guzman Baquiran(HGB)College is a private educational institution in Tumauini, Isabela. since its foundation in 2005, the school provide quality education that builds graduates who competent, active and adaptive in navigating problems in both the local and global comunities

HG Baquiran College is recognized by the commision on Higher Education (CHED) and the department of Education (DepEd). The mainly offers undergraduate programs in Teacher Education, Business Administration, Criminology, Information Technology, and Hotel and restaurant Management.
