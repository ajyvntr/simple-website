"The road to success is paved with good intentions, your success, then, begins with HGBaquiran College."

Business Administration Department
The Business Administration Department offers courses in management, marketing, finance, and more, providing students with essential skills for success in the business world. The program combines theory with practical experience, preparing students for leadership roles and diverse career opportunities.
Criminology Department
The Criminology Department offers courses on crime, criminal law, and justice systems, preparing students for careers in law enforcement, criminal justice, and social services.
Hotel and Restaurant Management Department
The Hotel and Restaurant Management Department offers courses that cover hospitality operations, food service, customer service, and event planning. Students gain practical skills and industry knowledge, preparing them for careers in hotel management, restaurant operations, and event coordination.
Information Technology Department
Explore the future with IT. Master programming, networking, cybersecurity, and database management. Gain hands-on tech experience.
Education Department
The Education Department course offers a comprehensive exploration of key teaching principles, curriculum design, classroom management, and assessment strategies. It covers educational psychology, inclusive practices for students with disabilities, and the integration of technology to enhance learning experiences.
